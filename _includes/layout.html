Important Service Information

  • Due to technical issues with the Go2 Card plugin, the plugin has been removed from the server, and hence all tickets, ticket machines, faregates and readers are currently not functional. Faregates have been opened at all stations and travel is free network-wide in the meantime.
  • Due to the installation of new track and rolling stock, Metro Line M3 is closed until further notice. Consider using Bus Route 26 for stations between Fra Mauro St and Capital Stn inclusive, Commuter Line C1 for National Mall, and Metro Line M1 for Lighthouse Pt. Victory Column is a short walk away from Northshore on the M2 or National Mall on the C1. Hibbing Gardens and South St can be reached on foot from Fra Mauro St and Sergels Sq. We sincerely apologise for any inconvenience caused by the closure and will update everyone when trains are running again. Thank you for your understanding as we work to keep you moving.
  • All other services are operating as per normal.
